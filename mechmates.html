In the first week of the new uni year we did a Game Jam where the theme was 'Together'. Together with 4 other people we decided to make a local co-op game where two players must work together to control a mech and beat as many alien creatures until the timer runs out. Our team consisted of two artist, one sound designer and one other programmer.
