About Me

Hi, I'm Jibbe

Hi! I'm Jibbe, a game programmer from The Netherlands born in 2006, currently a Creative Media & Game Technologies student at Hanze.
I recently completed my first year of Creative Media and Game Technologies cum laude and I am currently striving to make more awesome games in year 2 of the programme.
I have a wide range of skills across programming, 2D and 3D art, and game design, but my main forte is in programming.
I love taking on challenges, learning new things and I always like to put some extra time in to refine my creations making sure that the creative vision is achieved in the best possible way. Whether it's writing code, making art assets, or designing game mechanics, I thrive on bringing a game to life.
Feel free to get in touch if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work. =)
