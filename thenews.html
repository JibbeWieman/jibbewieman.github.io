For the first block of my studies year 2 we were tasked to make a VR game for people 30-years into a by us imagined future. Our team went with the approach to show our and talk about this future world by making a game about managing a news broadcast 30-years into the future. Having predicted that the attention span of people will continue to reduce the news stories are all bite-sized (max 20 sec). We also talk about current events going on in the world in the news stories which the player can pick between to broadcast. Besides picking news stories which influence the world around the player, the player must also keep up the statistics of the news station (money, viewership & awareness raised), manage which ads get sent out and prevent the world around him/her to go to waist.
